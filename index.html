Features

  • Players receive $1000 every time they go past the starting point. If they stop at the starting point, they might get more.
  • Press "Increase Speed" or "Normal Speed"to change the speed of the game.
  • Press "Turn On Autoplay" to let the computer play for all players.

How To Play

  • Press the dice to roll it. AI players will roll the dice on their own.
  • Goal: Make everyone else bankrupt!
  • Every box triggers a certain event of the following types:
  • Real Estate: Players can purchase and upgrade real estates. Other players have to pay rent when they stop at someone else's real estate.
  • Fates and Chances: Randomly trigger a good event or a bad event
  • Property Tax: Pay a random amount of tax
  • Casino: Get 500 times the number shown on the dice
  • Free Money & Starting Point: Get a random amount of money
  • Jail: Stuck there for a random number of days
  • The Alps: Stuck there for a random number of days and spend a random amount of money
  • Airport: Spend a certain amount of money on transportation
  • Rent Mechanism: There are 4 levels: land, house, mansion and hotel. The higher the level, the higher the rent.
  • Bankruptcy: If a player owns less than $0, the player is bankrupt. All owned real estates will be confisticated and can be bought by other players.

Enjoy your game~
